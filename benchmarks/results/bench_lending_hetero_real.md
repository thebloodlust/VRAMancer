## What the table is showing

- ``Pool active`` reflects whether ``InferencePipeline.lending_pool`` was actually instantiated (gated by ``num_gpus > 1`` and ``backend_type not in ('vllm', 'llamacpp')``).
- ``VRAM Δ`` is the post-bench minus pre-load delta per physical GPU. When lending is active, the pool reserves a buffer on the lender — VRAM Δ on the lender increases without the model being pinned there.
- The data plane underneath the lending pool uses ``TransferManager`` (Rust P2P bypass for ≥ 512 KB activations, Strategy 2 pipelined double-buffer otherwise — see ``benchmarks/bench_kv_migration.py`` and ``benchmarks/bench_transfer_strategies.py``). ReBAR full-window (Strategy 1.7) was validated under a different Proxmox config in ``docs/reports/REBAR_PROXMOX_BENCHMARK.md`` but is not active on this host (BAR0 = 8-16 MB, see T2.3 in BENCHMARK_RESULTS.md).
- **tok/s note**: 2.48 tok/s is much lower than the 16.1 tok/s 2-GPU BF16 baseline (BENCHMARK_RESULTS.md). `total_leases_created: 0` — no actual lease/borrow happened during this short 32-token generation, so the gap isn't lending overhead; it's a different layer/memory placement under the lending-aware `max_memory` budget. Needs follow-up with longer generations (more KV pressure, actual leases) before drawing conclusions about lending's steady-state cost.
